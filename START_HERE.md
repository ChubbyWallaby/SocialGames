## 💡 Key Findings (Ultra-Quick Summary)

### The Opportunity
**"Async games for groups"** - No one ranks for this term. It's a white space. This is where Confide wins.

### The Pain Point
**Video call fatigue** - People explicitly search for "games without video" and "no camera games." Confide solves this.

### The Market
**Evergreen demand** - Games are searched for year-round with no seasonal dips. Consistent, reliable market.

### The Competition
**Fragmented** - Competitors own Werewolf (Imposter Game), parties (Plato), enterprise (FunJelly). No one owns "friends playing async on WhatsApp."

### The Angle
**Not "multiplayer entertainment platform"** - Real people search for "games when bored," "group chat games," "games without video." Use this language.
