## 🎬 META TAG TEMPLATES (Fill in & Go)

### Template: Problem-Solver
**Title:** "[Problem]? Play These [Type] Games on WhatsApp"  
**Meta:** "Keep [audience] engaged without [pain point]. [Number]+ games ready to play. No app download, no setup."

*Examples:*
- Title: "Group Chat Dying? 50+ Games to Play on WhatsApp"
- Meta: "Keep your friend group engaged without video calls. 50+ social deduction games you can play right now."

### Template: How-To
**Title:** "How to Play [Game] on WhatsApp (Async Version)"  
**Meta:** "Step-by-step guide to playing [Game] asynchronously. Works across time zones, no scheduling needed."

*Examples:*
- Title: "How to Play Werewolf on WhatsApp (Async Version)"
- Meta: "Play Werewolf with friends in different time zones. No video call needed, no scheduling headaches."
