# Game 7: The Double Life

*Inspired by: The Circle, Catfish*

---

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Players** | 5-8 |
| **Duration** | 5-6 days |
| **Eliminations** | Yes — 1 "blocked" per day by top-rated players |
| **Confrontation** | Medium |
| **Best for** | Extroverts, writers, people who enjoy performing a character |
| **Core tension** | "Is this person real, or are they playing me?" |

---

## The Concept

Players create a profile — either their real identity or a completely fake persona (a "Catfish"). Each day, the host posts a prompt. Players answer in character. Everyone rates each other.

The top-rated players become "Influencers" and decide who to block (eliminate). Blocked players are revealed — real identity shown.

Catfish who survive to the end (undetected) get bonus points. Being genuine has its own strength — people tend to rate authenticity higher.

---

## Setup (Host Instructions)

### Before Day 1: Profile Creation

DM each player:
> **Create Your Profile**
>
> You have a choice:
> - **Play as yourself** — real name, real personality, real photo
> - **Play as a Catfish** — fake name, fake personality, any photo you choose
>
> DM me:
> 1. Display name (real or fake)
> 2. A 2-3 sentence bio
> 3. One profile photo (real selfie OR any image representing your character)
> 4. Your choice: REAL or CATFISH
>
> If Catfish: commit to this persona for the entire game. Break character = instant suspicion.
>
> Nobody else will know your choice until the game ends.

### Welcome Message (after collecting all profiles)

Post to group:
> 🎭 **Welcome to The Double Life**
>
> Meet the players:
>
> 🟢 **[Name 1]** — "[Bio]"
> 🟢 **[Name 2]** — "[Bio]"
> 🟢 **[Name 3]** — "[Bio]"
> 🟢 **[Name 4]** — "[Bio]"
> 🟢 **[Name 5]** — "[Bio]"
> [... etc.]
>
> Some are exactly who they say they are. Others are... performing.
>
> **How it works:**
> - Each day: a prompt. Answer in character.
> - Each evening: rate every player 1-5.
> - Top 2 rated = today's **Influencers**. They choose 1 player to **block**.
> - Blocked player's true identity is revealed. They leave the game.
>
> **Winning:** Last 2-3 standing face a final rating from ALL players (including eliminated ones).
> Highest-rated overall = winner.
> **Catfish bonus:** If a Catfish reaches the final with an average rating above 3.5 → bonus points.
>
> First prompt drops tomorrow at 09:00. Play your part. 🎭

## Daily Cycle

```
09:00       — Host posts daily prompt
09:00-17:00 — Players answer + social interaction (8 hours)
17:00-20:00 — Rating window: DM host your ratings for each player (1-5)
20:00       — Host calculates. Top 2 = Influencers (announced)
20:00-22:00 — Influencers discuss privately (DM each other). Agree on 1 player to block.
22:00       — Blocked player revealed. True identity shown.
```

---

## Rating System

Each player DMs host their ratings:
> "My ratings today:
> [Name 1]: 4
> [Name 2]: 2
> [Name 3]: 5
> [Name 4]: 3
> [Name 5]: 1"

**Criteria (shared with players):**
> Rate each player 1-5 based on:
> - How genuine and authentic they feel
> - How engaging and interesting their answers are
> - How much you'd want to be friends with them
>
> You cannot rate yourself.

**Ties for Influencer:** If players are tied for 2nd place, ALL tied players become Influencers and must agree unanimously on the block. If they can't agree in 2 hours, the highest-rated Influencer decides alone.

---

## Influencer Blocking

When announced as Influencers:
> "⭐ Today's Influencers: [Name A] and [Name B]!
>
> You have 2 hours to privately agree on 1 player to block.
> DM each other. When you've decided, both DM me the same name.
> If you can't agree: [highest-rated] gets final say."

After blocking decision:
> "🚫 [Name] has been BLOCKED.
>
> Their true identity: [Real name / Catfish reveal]
> [If Catfish: 'They were playing a character the entire time. Their real name is [X].']
> [If Real: 'They were being completely genuine. What you saw was real.']
>
> [Name]'s final message: [player gets to post one goodbye]"

## Daily Prompts (one per day)

These should test personality, opinions, and storytelling — making it harder for Catfish to stay consistent but rewarding genuine players.

**Day 1: Introduction**
> "Beyond your bio — tell us something about yourself that's hard to explain in 2 sentences. What makes you, you?"

**Day 2: Vulnerability**
> "What's your most embarrassing moment? The kind of story you only tell people you trust."

**Day 3: Evidence**
> "Show us something real. Post a photo of: your desk, your breakfast, your view, or your hands. (Catfish: post what fits your character.)"

**Day 4: Opinion**
> "Hot take time. Share a genuinely unpopular opinion you hold. Something that might make people rate you lower — but you believe it anyway."

**Day 5: Voice (if comfortable)**
> "Send a 15-second voice note: tell us your name and one thing you're grateful for today."
> (Catfish can decline — but declining is suspicious. Or they can find a friend to record for them.)

**Day 6 (Final): Appeal**
> "Final day. Why should people rate you highly? Make your case. Are you real? Are you worth trusting?"

> 🏆 **Final Ratings**
>
> [2-3 names] remain. Time for the ultimate rating.
>
> ALL players — including eliminated ones — now rate the finalists one last time (1-5).
> This is the rating that decides the winner.
>
> Eliminated players: you've been watching. You know things the finalists don't know you know.
>
> DM me your final ratings within 8 hours.

> 🎭 **The Double Life — Final Reveal**
>
> **Final ratings:**
> - [Name A]: Average 4.2
> - [Name B]: Average 3.8
> - [Name C]: Average 3.5

>

>

## Scoring

| Achievement | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| Win (highest final rating) | 10 |
| Runner-up | 7 |
| Third place | 5 |
| Catfish reaches final + rating > 3.5 | +3 bonus |
| Catfish reaches final + rating > 4.0 | +5 bonus |
| Real player with highest overall average | +2 bonus |
| Correctly predicted a Catfish in chat (publicly, before reveal) | +1 |

## Tips for Hosts

- **Don't reveal Catfish ratios.** Players shouldn't know how many Catfish are in the game (could be 0, could be half).
- **Encourage organic conversation** between prompts. The game is richer when players chat naturally, not just answer prompts. Post: "Free time — chat, bond, investigate. This counts toward tonight's ratings."
- **Blocked reveals are the drama peaks.** Build anticipation: "🚫 The Influencers have decided..." [wait 5 minutes] "...[Name] has been blocked." Then: the identity reveal. These moments make the game.
- **Voice notes on Day 5 are optional but powerful.** Catfish players face a real dilemma: refuse (suspicious) or commit fully (find someone to record as their character). Let them squirm.
- **The Jury effect:** Eliminated players watching silently adds a layer. They know things, they've been revealed, and they get to vote in the final rating. Remind finalists: "Everyone who was blocked is watching. They remember how you treated them."
- **Catfish advice (share via DM):** "Pick a persona close to your real personality but different enough to be interesting. Don't be an entirely different person — consistency is too hard over 5 days. Change one or two things (age, job, location) and keep everything else close to real."
