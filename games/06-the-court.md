# Game 6: The Court

*Inspired by: Game of Thrones, Diplomacy, political intrigue*

---

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Players** | 6-8 |
| **Duration** | 6-7 days |
| **Eliminations** | Yes (via assassination — but random, not targeted voting) |
| **Confrontation** | Very High |
| **Best for** | Drama lovers, schemers, players who enjoy complex multi-layered social strategy |
| **Core tension** | "Everyone has a different goal. I don't know what anyone actually wants." |

---

## The Concept

Players are nobles in a fictional royal court. One player starts as King and can issue daily decrees that change the rules. Everyone has a **secret agenda** (personal win condition). Alliances shift constantly because no two players want the same thing.

Each night, players choose an action: Assassinate, Protect, Overthrow, Spy, or Pass. Actions resolve simultaneously and create chaos.

You don't need to survive to win. You need to fulfill YOUR agenda.

---

## Setup (Host Instructions)

1. Create WhatsApp group
2. Randomly select one player as starting King (DM them)
3. DM each player a Secret Agenda from the list below
4. Post welcome message

### Welcome Message (copy-paste to group)

> 👑 **Welcome to The Court**
>
> You are nobles in the Royal Court of Valdoria. One of you is King. The rest serve, plot, and wait.
>
> **How it works:**
> - Each morning, The King issues a **Decree** (a rule that changes gameplay for the day)
> - Each evening, every player secretly submits one **Action** via DM
> - Actions resolve overnight. Results posted the next morning.
>
> **Your King:** [Player Name] 👑
>
> **Important:** Each of you has a **secret win condition** (your Agenda). You do NOT all want the same thing. You might not even need to survive to win.
>
> **Actions available each night:**
> - ⚔️ Assassinate [player] — 50% chance of success
> - 🛡️ Protect [player or self] — blocks one assassination on that target
> - 🔄 Overthrow — removes the King (needs 3+ players to submit this same night)
> - 🔍 Spy on [player] — learn a keyword from their secret agenda
> - ⏭️ Pass — do nothing
>
> The court is open. Day 1 begins tomorrow.
>
> Crowns are heavy. Daggers are sharp. Trust is expensive. 🗡️

## Daily Cycle

```
09:00       — Host posts: Morning announcements (last night's results)
09:30       — The King posts their daily Decree
10:00-20:00 — Discussion, scheming, alliance-building (10 hours)
20:00-22:00 — Action Window: each player DMs host ONE action
22:00       — Host resolves all actions
             Posts results next morning at 09:00
```

## Actions (Detailed)

Each player submits exactly ONE action per night via DM to host.

### ⚔️ Assassinate [target]
- 50% chance of success (host flips a coin or uses random.org)
- If **successful:** target is eliminated. Announced next morning.
- If **failed:** target is told (privately, via DM) that someone attempted to kill them — but NOT who. The attacker's identity stays secret.
- You cannot assassinate the King (must Overthrow them instead).

### 🛡️ Protect [target or self]
- Blocks ONE assassination attempt on that player tonight.
- If no one tried to assassinate the target, the protection is "wasted" (no announcement).
- If the target WAS attacked: "An assassination attempt on [target] was blocked by a protector."
- You cannot protect yourself two nights in a row.

### 🔄 Overthrow
- Only works if **3 or more** players submit Overthrow on the same night.
- If threshold met: King is deposed. All overthrowers privately vote (DM host) for a new King among themselves. Majority wins.
- If threshold NOT met: Overthrow fails. King is told "An overthrow was attempted but failed." The specific players are NOT revealed.
- The King cannot submit Overthrow (they're already King).

### 🔍 Spy on [target]
- Host DMs you ONE keyword from the target's secret agenda.
- Example: if target's agenda is "Become King by Day 5," you might receive the word "King" or "Day 5."
- You get a fragment, not the full agenda. Combine multiple spy results to deduce.

### ⏭️ Pass
- Do nothing. Sometimes the safest move.
- No announcement is made.

## The King's Power: Decrees

The King posts ONE decree per day (in the group chat) that changes the rules for that day/night.

**Decree examples (pick from this list or invent your own):**

1. "**Decree: Transparency** — All actions tonight are revealed publicly (including who did what)."
2. "**Decree: Lockdown** — No assassinations allowed tonight. All assassination attempts automatically fail."
3. "**Decree: Paranoia** — Tonight, Protect only works if you protect YOURSELF. You cannot protect others."
4. "**Decree: The Court Speaks** — Every player must post at least one message in the group today or lose their action tonight."
5. "**Decree: Double Edge** — Assassination success rate tonight is 75% instead of 50%. High risk, high reward."
6. "**Decree: Exile** — I name [Player]. If 2+ players also vote Overthrow tonight, [Player] is exiled (eliminated) instead of me."
7. "**Decree: Silence** — The first player to send a message in the group today loses their action tonight. Choose your words carefully."
8. "**Decree: Pardon** — [Player] cannot be assassinated tonight. Royal protection."
9. "**Decree: Taxation** — Each player must DM me one hint about their agenda (one word or phrase). Refusal = forfeit tonight's action."
10. "**Decree: Revelation** — I reveal one Spy result publicly: [Player X]'s agenda contains the word '[keyword].'"

**Rules for Kings:**
- Must post decree by 10:00 (1 hour after results). If they don't, host posts "The King issued no decree today. All normal rules apply."
- Decrees cannot directly eliminate a player (except Exile, which still requires group cooperation).
- Decrees last ONE day only unless stated otherwise.

## Secret Agendas (assign one per player)

### For 6 players, pick 6 of these:

**1. The Survivor**
> Win condition: Be alive on the final day. That's it. Don't die.

**2. The Kingmaker**
> Win condition: A player you helped install as King (via Overthrow participation) is still King on the final day. You yourself must NOT be King.

**3. The Assassin**
> Win condition: Successfully assassinate 2 specific players (host assigns targets at start). You don't need to survive — just get both kills.

**4. The Pacifist**
> Win condition: Three nights pass with ZERO successful assassinations (not necessarily consecutive). Your Protection actions are your greatest weapon.

**5. The Usurper**
> Win condition: Become King by Day 5 AND hold the crown for 2 consecutive days.

**6. The Spy Master**
> Win condition: Correctly identify 3 other players' exact agendas. DM host your guesses by Day 6. (Use Spy actions + deduction.)

**7. The Puppet Master**
> Win condition: Get 3 different players to target someone YOU suggested (in group chat or private DMs). They don't need to know you influenced them.

**8. The Martyr**
> Win condition: Get yourself assassinated. You WANT to die — but you can't make it obvious, or no one will waste their action on you.

## Endgame

The game ends at the end of Day 6 (or Day 7 for 8-player games).

**Final morning, host posts:**
> 👑 **The Court Closes**
>
> The game is over. Let's see who achieved their agenda.
>
> Each player: DM me — do you believe you've completed your win condition? State your case.
>
> After review, I'll reveal all agendas and announce winners.

**Multiple winners are possible.** (The Survivor and The Kingmaker can both win. The Assassin and The Pacifist probably can't both win.)

Post full reveal:
> **Agenda Reveals:**
> - [Player A]: The Survivor — ✅ Survived to final day. **WINS.**
> - [Player B]: The Assassin (targets: C and E) — ✅ Both eliminated. **WINS.**
> - [Player C]: The Usurper — ❌ Never became King. **LOSES.**
> - ...

## Tips for Hosts

- **Action resolution order matters.** Resolve in this order each night:
  1. Overthrow check (did 3+ submit?)
  2. Protection (mark protected players)
  3. Assassination attempts (50% roll for each, blocked if protected)
  4. Spy results (DM back to spy)
- **The King should be a talker.** If your randomly-assigned King is very quiet, the game will be slower. Consider assigning King to a naturally social player.
- **Post dramatic results.** Don't just list outcomes. Narrate:
  > "☀️ Dawn breaks over Valdoria. The court assembles... but [Player]'s chair is empty. In the night, a blade found its mark. [Player] has been assassinated."
- **Encourage scheming in DMs.** Alliances in this game are necessarily private. Remind players: "Your DMs are your war room. Plot freely."
- **Track everything.** You need to know: who's King, who's alive, all spy results given, assassination attempts (successful and failed), and who's submitted Overthrow historically.
- **Multiple winners = good.** 2-3 players winning simultaneously is common and expected. The drama is in WHICH agendas conflict.

## 🤖 Bot Variant

The Court is fully bot-compatible. All player actions are structured commands, all resolution is deterministic or random (coin flips), and agenda tracking is data-driven.

### What Changes

- **Action submission** uses structured commands the bot parses
- **Resolution** is fully automated (protection → assassination rolls → overthrow threshold → spy keywords)
- **King decrees** must be selected from a numbered list (bot enforces the decree's effect)
- **Spy results** are auto-generated from keyword extraction
- **Agenda tracking** is automated — bot checks win conditions each morning

### Bot Action System

**Each night (20:00-22:00), players DM the bot ONE action:**

| Action | Command Format |
|--------|---------------|
| Assassinate | `ASSASSINATE @PlayerName` |
| Protect | `PROTECT @PlayerName` or `PROTECT SELF` |
| Overthrow | `OVERTHROW` |
| Spy | `SPY @PlayerName` |
| Pass | `PASS` |

**Bot validation:**
- Can't assassinate the King → "❌ You cannot assassinate the King. Use OVERTHROW instead."
- Can't protect self two nights in a row → "❌ You protected yourself last night. Choose a different target."
- King can't overthrow → "❌ You are the King. You cannot overthrow yourself."
- Dead player tries to act → "❌ You have been eliminated and cannot submit actions."
- Invalid player name → "❌ I don't recognize that player. Active players: [list]."
- Confirmation → "✅ Action received: [action]. You can change your action until 22:00 by sending a new command."

### Bot Resolution Order

At 22:00, bot resolves automatically:

1. **Overthrow check:** Count OVERTHROW submissions. If ≥ 3 → King deposed. Bot DMs all overthrowers: "Overthrow successful! Vote for new King: reply with a player name from: [list of overthrowers]." Majority among overthrowers = new King. If < 3 → Bot DMs King: "An overthrow was attempted but failed."

2. **Protection:** Mark all PROTECT targets as protected for this night.

3. **Assassination:** For each ASSASSINATE action:
   - Is target protected? → Blocked. Bot posts: "🛡️ An assassination attempt was blocked by a protector."
   - Not protected → Bot generates random number (1-100). ≤ 50 = success, > 50 = fail.
   - Success → target eliminated. Queued for morning announcement.
   - Fail → Bot DMs target: "⚠️ Someone attempted to assassinate you tonight. They failed. You don't know who."

4. **Spy:** For each SPY action, bot extracts one keyword from target's agenda text and DMs it to the spy: "🔍 Spy result: [Target]'s agenda contains the word '[keyword].'"

### Bot Decree System

The King must select a decree by number instead of writing free-form. Bot DMs the King each morning:

> 👑 **Issue your Decree for today.**
>
> Reply with a number:
> 1. Transparency — all actions revealed publicly tonight
> 2. Lockdown — no assassinations tonight
> 3. Paranoia — protect only works on yourself tonight
> 4. The Court Speaks — players must post in group today or lose their action
> 5. Double Edge — assassination success rate = 75% tonight
> 6. Exile [name] — reply `6 @PlayerName` — if 2+ also overthrow, that player is exiled
> 7. Silence — first player to message in group loses their action
> 8. Pardon [name] — reply `8 @PlayerName` — that player can't be assassinated tonight
> 9. Taxation — each player must DM you one hint about their agenda, or forfeit action
> 10. Revelation — reply `10 @PlayerName keyword` — reveal a spy result publicly
>
> Reply by 10:00 or no decree is issued.

**Bot enforces decree effects:**
- Decree 1 (Transparency): bot reveals all actions in the morning announcement instead of keeping them secret.
- Decree 2 (Lockdown): all ASSASSINATE actions automatically fail.
- Decree 5 (Double Edge): assassination threshold changes to ≤ 75 instead of ≤ 50.
- Decree 7 (Silence): bot monitors group chat; first message sender is flagged; their action is ignored during resolution.
- Decree 4 (The Court Speaks): bot tracks who posted in group chat; non-posters' actions are ignored.

### Bot Agenda Checking

Each morning, bot checks all agendas against game state:

| Agenda | Bot Check |
|--------|-----------|
| The Survivor | Is this player still alive? (Check on final day) |
| The Kingmaker | Did this player participate in an overthrow that installed the current King? Is current King still King on final day? Is this player NOT King? |
| The Assassin | Were both assigned targets successfully assassinated by this player? |
| The Pacifist | Have 3 nights passed with 0 successful assassinations? |
| The Usurper | Did this player become King by Day 5? Have they held it for 2 consecutive days? |
| The Spy Master | Did this player DM correct agenda guesses for 3+ players? (Checked on final day) |
| The Puppet Master | Did 3 different players target someone this player suggested? (Requires chat analysis — see note) |
| The Martyr | Was this player assassinated? |

**Note on The Puppet Master:** This agenda requires analyzing chat for influence, which is hard for a bot. In bot mode, replace with:

**The Puppet Master (Bot Version):**
> Win condition: Submit 3 correct predictions of other players' nightly actions. Each night, before submitting your own action, you may also DM: `PREDICT @Player ACTION`. If correct, it counts toward your win condition.

### Bot Spy Keyword Extraction

Bot stores each agenda as a text string. When a spy action targets a player, the bot randomly selects one significant word from their agenda (skipping common words like "the", "a", "and", "is", "on", "by").

Example: Agenda = "Become King by Day 5 AND hold the crown for 2 consecutive days"
Keyword pool: ["Become", "King", "Day", "hold", "crown", "consecutive"]
Bot randomly picks one and DMs the spy.

### Bot Message Formats

**Player commands:**
- Action: `ASSASSINATE @Ana`, `PROTECT SELF`, `OVERTHROW`, `SPY @Bruno`, `PASS`
- Overthrow vote: `@PlayerName` (when prompted)
- Spy Master guess: `AGENDA @Player: The Survivor`
- Puppet Master predict: `PREDICT @Ana ASSASSINATE @Bruno`

**King commands:**
- Decree: `3` (for Paranoia), `6 @Ana` (for Exile Ana), `8 @Bruno` (for Pardon Bruno)
