# Game 5: The Auction

*Inspired by: Pit, trading card games, stock market simulations*

---

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Players** | 5-7 |
| **Duration** | 5 days |
| **Eliminations** | None — everyone trades all week |
| **Confrontation** | Medium |
| **Best for** | Analytical players, negotiators, people who enjoy deal-making and bluffing |
| **Core tension** | "Why does she want my Gold so badly? What does she know that I don't?" |

---

## The Concept

Players hold resource cards and trade with each other to fulfill daily contracts (combinations that pay out). Trades happen freely via group chat and private DMs — lying about what you hold is allowed.

One player — **The Insider** — secretly sees tomorrow's contract 12 hours before everyone else. They can trade for tomorrow's resources while others don't know their value yet.

At the end: richest player wins. Then the group votes on who The Insider is — if caught, The Insider loses half their earnings.

---

## Setup (Host Instructions)

1. Create WhatsApp group
2. Assign starting resources to each player (DM):
   - Each player gets 3 random resource cards from: 🔴 Red, 🔵 Blue, 🟢 Green, 🟡 Gold, ⚪ Silver
   - Use a random generator or draw from a shuffled list
3. Assign one player as The Insider (DM):
   > "You are The Insider. Each evening at 21:00, I'll DM you tomorrow's contract — 12 hours before the group sees it. Use this knowledge to trade smart."
4. Post welcome message

### Welcome Message (copy-paste to group)

> 💹 **Welcome to The Auction**
>
> Each day, a **Contract** is revealed. Contracts pay if you hold the right combination of resources.
>
> **Your starting hand:** (check your DM)
>
> **Rules:**
> - Trading: anytime, with anyone, in group chat or private DMs
> - Trades are binding once both players confirm to me (DM me: "Trade confirmed: I gave [X] to [Player] for [Y]")
> - You MAY lie about what you hold
> - You may NOT show screenshots of your DMs with me
> - Each evening, 1 new random resource card is added to each player's hand
>
> **The Insider:** One player sees tomorrow's contract tonight. They know what's valuable before you do.
>
> **End of game:** Richest player wins. Then: vote on who The Insider is.
> If caught → Insider loses 50% of earnings.
>
> Day 1 contract drops at 09:00 tomorrow. Start thinking about what you need.

## Daily Cycle

```
09:00       — Contract revealed to group
09:00-20:00 — Trading window (11 hours, fully async)
20:00       — Deadline: DM host your current hand (for payout calculation)
20:30       — Host calculates payouts, posts daily results
21:00       — Host distributes 1 new random card to each player
             — Host DMs The Insider tomorrow's contract (secret)
```

## Trading Rules

**What's allowed:**
- 1-for-1 trades ("My Red for your Blue")
- Unequal trades ("2 Greens for your 1 Gold")
- Futures ("I'll give you a card tomorrow if you give me Blue today")
- Favors ("I'll tell you what I know about [player]'s hand if you give me Silver")
- Bluffing ("I have 3 Golds" — when you don't)
- Group trades ("Anyone want to do a 3-way swap?")

**What's NOT allowed:**
- Showing screenshots of DMs with the host
- Reneging on a confirmed trade (once both players DM host "confirmed," it's final)
- Trading with the host (nice try)

**How to confirm a trade:**
Both players DM the host: "Trade confirmed: I gave [card] to [player] and received [card]."
Host updates both hands in their records.

## The Insider

**What they know:** Tomorrow's contract, 12 hours before the group.

**How this helps:** If tomorrow's contract rewards Gold, The Insider trades for Gold TODAY — when others don't know Gold will be valuable. By the time the contract is revealed, they already hold what's needed.

**Detection clues:**
- Does someone always seem to have exactly what the contract needs?
- Who trades aggressively the evening before a reveal?
- Who offers "bad" trades that turn out to be genius in hindsight?

## Tips for Hosts

- **Track hands carefully.** Use a spreadsheet or notes app. Every trade changes two hands. Don't lose track.
- **Day 4 (The Hoarder) creates chaos.** Suddenly quantity matters, not combos. Players who traded away "junk" cards regret it. This is intentional — it punishes The Insider who hoarded specific cards.
- **Announce approximate hand sizes daily** (not exact contents): "Current hand sizes: A:4, B:3, C:5, D:3, E:4" — this creates trading dynamics without revealing holdings.
- **Encourage group chat trades.** Private DMs for deals are fine, but the game is more fun when negotiation happens publicly. Post a "Market Open" message each morning.
- **The Insider is harder to detect than you think.** Players who are naturally good traders look identical to someone with insider info. That's what makes the final vote interesting.

## 🤖 Bot Variant

The Auction is the most bot-ready game in the collection. All core mechanics — hand tracking, trades, payouts, card distribution — are numeric and structured. This variant requires minimal changes.

### What Changes

- **Trade confirmation** uses a structured message format the bot can parse
- **Hand tracking** is fully automated (no spreadsheet needed)
- **Payout calculation** is instant at deadline
- **Insider info delivery** is scheduled automatically
- **Card distribution** (daily random card) is automated

### Bot Trade System

**To propose a trade (in group chat or DM to another player):**
Players negotiate freely in natural language. The bot doesn't need to understand negotiation — it only processes confirmations.

**To confirm a trade (both players DM the bot):**
```
TRADE @PlayerName GIVE Red RECEIVE Blue
```

**Bot logic:**
1. Receives trade confirmation from Player A.
2. Waits for matching confirmation from Player B (must match: same cards, reversed direction).
3. If both match → trade executes. Bot DMs both: "✅ Trade complete. Your updated hand: [list]."
4. If mismatch → bot DMs both: "❌ Trade details don't match. Please resubmit."
5. If only one player confirms within 1 hour → bot DMs both: "⏰ Trade not confirmed by both parties. Cancelled."

**Bot validates:** Does the player actually hold the card they're giving? If not: "❌ You don't have a Red card to trade."

### Bot Hand Management

**Check your hand anytime:** DM the bot `HAND` → bot replies with your current cards.

**Daily card distribution (21:00):** Bot randomly assigns 1 card from the pool to each player. DMs: "🃏 You received: [card]. Your hand: [full list]."

### Bot Payout Calculation

At 20:00 each day, the bot automatically checks each player's hand against the day's contract:

```
📜 Day 2 Contract: "The Rainbow" — 1 Red + 1 Blue + 1 Green = €600. Bonus Gold = +€200.

Results:
- Ana: Red, Blue, Green, Gold → €600 + €200 = €800 ✅
- Bruno: Red, Red, Blue → €0
- Carla: Blue, Green, Silver → €0
- David: Red, Blue, Green → €600 ✅
```

No submission needed — the bot already knows every player's hand.

### Bot Insider Mechanics

- Bot randomly selects Insider at game start.
- Each evening at 21:00, bot DMs the Insider: "📜 Tomorrow's contract: [contract details]. Trade wisely."
- All other players receive only: "🃏 You received: [new card]. Your hand: [list]."

### Bot Endgame

After Day 5 payouts:

1. Bot posts final earnings leaderboard.
2. Bot DMs each player: "Who is The Insider? Reply with one player name."
3. Deadline: 6 hours.
4. Bot tallies votes. Majority correct = Insider caught (loses 50%).
5. Bot posts final reveal + adjusted scores.

### Bot Message Formats

**Player commands (DM to bot):**
- Check hand: `HAND`
- Confirm trade: `TRADE @Bruno GIVE Red RECEIVE Gold`
- Multi-card trade: `TRADE @Bruno GIVE Red,Red RECEIVE Gold`
- Insider vote: `@PlayerName`

**Bot responses:**
- Hand check: "🃏 Your hand: Red, Blue, Gold, Silver (4 cards)"
- Trade confirmed: "✅ Trade complete. You gave Red to Bruno, received Gold. Hand: Blue, Gold, Gold, Silver"
- Payout: "💰 Day 3: You qualify for Precious Metals (2 Gold + 1 Silver). Payout: €800. Total earnings: €1,400."
- No payout: "💰 Day 3: You don't qualify for today's contract. Payout: €0. Total earnings: €600."
