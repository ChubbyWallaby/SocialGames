## 📱 META TAG TEMPLATES (Copy & Customize)

### Template 1: Problem-Solution
**Title:** "[Problem] - [Solution] on WhatsApp"
- Example: "Group Chat Dying? Play These Social Deduction Games on WhatsApp"
- Example: "Video Call Fatigue? The Best Text-Based Games for Remote Friends"

**Meta Description:** "[Benefit] + [Mechanism] + [Social Proof]"
- Example: "Keep friends engaged without video. Play 50+ social deduction games entirely in WhatsApp chat. Join 100K+ players."

### Template 2: List Format (High Click-Through)
**Title:** "[Number]+ Best [Adjective] [Game Type] Games for [Context]"
- Example: "60+ Best Social Deduction Games for WhatsApp Groups"
- Example: "40+ Async Party Games You Can Play Right Now"

**Meta Description:** "Complete list of [game types] you can play on WhatsApp. [Key benefit]. Includes rules, examples & scoring."

### Template 3: How-To (Featured Snippet)
**Title:** "How to Play [Game Name] on WhatsApp (Async Version)"
- Example: "How to Play Werewolf on WhatsApp Without Everyone Online"
- Example: "How to Play Mafia on WhatsApp: Complete Async Guide"

**Meta Description:** "Step-by-step guide to playing [game] asynchronously. Download rules, customize for your group, start today."

### Template 4: Comparison (Authoritative)
**Title:** "[Game A] vs [Game B]: Which Social Deduction Game is Best for WhatsApp?"
- Example: "Werewolf vs Mafia: Which Works Better on WhatsApp?"
- Example: "Imposter Game vs Spyfall: Social Deduction Comparison"

**Meta Description:** "Compare [Game A] and [Game B] for WhatsApp groups. Pros, cons, rules, and which suits your group best."

## 🔍 Featured Snippet Targets (Write for These)

### "What is..."
**Target:** "What is async gaming?"
**Answer Structure:** 2-3 sentence definition + why it matters + example
**Use in:** Blog intro paragraphs, glossary pages

### "How do you..."
**Target:** "How do you play Werewolf on WhatsApp without everyone online?"
**Answer Structure:** Step-by-step numbered list (5-7 steps)
**Use in:** How-to blog posts, FAQ pages

### "How many..."
**Target:** "How many social deduction games can you play?"
**Answer Structure:** "You can play 50+ variations, but [top 5] are most popular"
**Use in:** Listicle introductions

### "Can you..."
**Target:** "Can you play Mafia asynchronously?"
**Answer Structure:** "Yes, here's how: [mechanism explained] + benefits + comparison to sync version"
**Use in:** FAQ sections, blog conclusions
